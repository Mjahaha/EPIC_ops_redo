Team Dashboard


This dashboard would mirror My Work, but at a team level so you can see workload and progress across everyone in your group.


It will highlight who has which actions and cases, how long they have held them, and where items may be blocked so you can spot bottlenecks quickly.


Expect filters and pivots similar to My Work, plus team rollups (by officer, case type, priority) to help reallocate work and keep things moving.
